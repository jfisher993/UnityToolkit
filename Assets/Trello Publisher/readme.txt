1) To get your Key go to:  https://trello.com/app-key

2) To get your Token go to: https://trello.com/1/authorize?key={YOUR_KEY}&scope=read%2Cwrite&expiration=never&response_type=token

3) To get your Board id's go to: https://api.trello.com/1/members/me/boards?key={YOUR_KEY}&token={YOUR_TOKEN}

4) To get your List id's go to:
https://api.trello.com/1/boards/{BOARD_ID}/lists?key={YOUR_KEY}&token={YOUR_TOKEN}
